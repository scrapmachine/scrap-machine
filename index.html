Mo Blue is a Canadian, multidisciplinary artist and iconoclast whom bends time and dimension by way of sound. His new release under Scrap Machine entitled Junk FM unearths liminal space, and affords room to express and cleanse therein. Work 1 and Work 2 serve to deconstruct art-pop, and it illicits catharsis upon its listeners. Work 3 is the act of tuning a station, tuning- or searching. Work 4 explores the darker recess of emotion with audible sounds of wailing. Work 5 and Work 6 masterfully concludes Junk FM's voyage into the unknown with dissonance and western strums. Kick off your shoes, find ‘empty’ space and listen to Junk FM. - Andra Durham
